About LockinRa

LockinRa is a local-first focus and productivity app: capture tasks (by voice or text), run focus sessions with distraction reminders, track habits, take notes, and play focus soundscapes — all stored on your own device.

  • Works offline; no account required.
  • On-device voice transcription and on-device AI by default.
  • Optional integrations (Google, Microsoft, GitHub, Slack) and cloud AI are opt-in.
